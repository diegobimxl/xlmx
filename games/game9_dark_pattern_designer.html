BIAS REVEALED: Anchoring

You anchored users on the bright "Stay" button. The Anchoring Bias means the first piece of information seen becomes the reference point for all decisions. The "Delete" option existed — it was legal — but anchored so far from the user's attention it might as well not have. You didn't deceive them. You just controlled what they noticed first.
