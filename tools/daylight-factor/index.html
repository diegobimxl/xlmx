Daylight Factor + WFR Calculator

Simplified Daylight Factor: DF = (Aw/Af) x VT x Refl x correction. WFR = Aw/Af. References: IES HB-10 Recommended Illuminance, LEED v4 EQc7 Daylight (min 300 lux / 28 fc for 55% of regularly occupied area), ASHRAE 189.1 and California Title 24 daylighting mandates.
