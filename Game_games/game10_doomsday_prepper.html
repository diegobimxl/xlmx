You Didn't Make It

BIAS REVEALED: Availability Heuristic

You prepped for what felt most likely — based on what you'd heard about most recently. This is the Availability Heuristic: we judge the probability of events by how easily examples come to mind. The headlines made certain threats feel urgent. But the actual threat rolled a die. Human brains are terrible probability estimators.
